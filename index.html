Projects

Towny Spawn Menu

Towny Spawn Menu is a Towny addon which adds a simple and cool menu to browse through all your server's nations and towns in a breeze. Guess what, you can teleport to them by clicking too!

Animated Nicknames

Animated Nicknames is a plugin which adds style to your in-game name by adding nice animations both in the player list (tab).

Filtering Hoppers

Filtering Hoppers is a free and open source alternative to the many other hopper utility plugins already out there.

Stats Editor

Stats Editor allows you to edit in-game statistics which affect many different things like how many phantoms spawn above you at night, or how often.

No More Bats

No More Bats prevents every type of bats from spawning into your world. Have fun exploring and mining without being attacked by those pesky flying rats every 5 seconds!

No More Worms

No More Worms prevents every type of worms from spawning into your world. Have fun exploring and mining without being attacked by those annoying digging rats every 5 seconds!
